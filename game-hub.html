War Tanks

Engage in epic tank warfare right in your browser! War Tanks is a classic 2-player artillery game where mathematics and trajectory prediction are your greatest weapons. Choose from a massive arsenal of weapons ranging from standard armor-piercing shells to massive terrain-destroying dirt movers. Set your angle, select your power, and blast your opponent into oblivion.

Every match takes place on procedurally generated terrain, meaning no two battles are ever the same. You must account for hills, valleys, and deep craters to ensure your shot lands perfectly.

Why Play War Tanks?

Because it perfectly blends strategic thinking with instant competitive action. It's the ideal game to play against a friend during a quick break.

Hex Blast

Hex Blast is a mesmerizing hexagonal puzzle game where you tap clusters of matching hexagons to blast them away. The bigger the cluster, the higher your score. Chain reactions multiply your points exponentially — find a 10-hex cluster and watch the fireworks explode.

With a clean, modern aesthetic and satisfying particle effects, Hex Blast is the perfect game for quick brain-teasing sessions. Each level introduces more colors, increasing the strategic depth as you plan your moves carefully.

Why Play Hex Blast?

Because finding that massive 12-hex cluster and watching it explode in a shower of glowing particles is deeply satisfying. It's the perfect blend of strategy and instant gratification.

  • ✓ Hexagonal Grid with Chain Combos
  • ✓ Progressive Difficulty Levels
  • ✓ Satisfying Particle Effects

Arcade / Runner Live

Sky Dodge

Sky Dodge is a fast-paced endless arcade runner where you control a glowing orb hurtling through an increasingly dangerous field of obstacles. Red barriers, amber orbs, and purple walls fly towards you at ever-increasing speeds. One hit and it's game over.

Green power-up orbs scattered through the chaos give you bonus points and a brief moment of relief. The procedurally generated obstacle patterns mean no two runs are ever the same, making each attempt uniquely challenging.

Why Play Sky Dodge?

It's the quintessential "one more try" game. Simple controls (just arrow keys or touch), but deceptively hard to master. Perfect for competing against your own high score.

  • ✓ Procedurally Generated Obstacles
  • ✓ Progressive Speed Ramping
  • ✓ Power-ups and Score Multipliers

Action / Multiplayer Live

Orb Clash

Orb Clash is a thrilling 2-player local multiplayer physics arena. Each player controls a glowing orb and shoots bouncing projectiles at their opponent. Projectiles ricochet off the arena walls, gaining extra damage with each bounce — making ricochets more rewarding than direct shots.

Matches are played in a best-of-5 rounds format. Player collision physics add another layer of strategy — you can ram your opponent into incoming fire or use your momentum to dodge attacks at the last second.

Why Play Orb Clash?

It's the ultimate couch multiplayer experience. Grab a friend, split the keyboard, and compete in intense physics-based arena combat. Simple to learn, but the bouncing projectile mechanics create incredible depth.

  • ✓ 2-Player Local Multiplayer
  • ✓ Physics-Based Projectile Combat
  • ✓ Best-of-5 Round System
