About Antigravity

Building tools that make productivity feel effortless

Our Mission

At Antigravity, we believe productivity shouldn't feel like a chore. We build beautifully designed apps that help you focus, build habits, and achieve your goals — all while making the process enjoyable.

Focus Flow was born from a simple idea: what if staying focused felt as rewarding as the work itself? By combining the proven Pomodoro technique with the satisfaction of growing a virtual forest, we created an app that turns productivity into a creative, visual experience.

🎯

Focus First

We design every feature to minimize distractions and maximize your deep work potential.

Beautiful Design

🌱

Growth Mindset

Visual progress through your growing forest keeps you motivated day after day.

What We Offer

  • Focus Timer — Pomodoro-based timer with customizable work and break intervals
  • Virtual Forest — Grow unique trees across 4 species and multiple seasons
  • Ambient Sounds — 10+ calming sounds categorized for focus and break sessions
  • Habit Tracker — Build and maintain daily habits with streaks and reminders
  • Statistics — Track your productivity trends with detailed analytics
  • Multi-language — Available in English, Hindi, Portuguese, and more

Our Values

Privacy — We respect your data. Focus Flow works entirely on your device; your productivity data stays with you.

Quality — Every pixel, every animation, every sound is crafted with care to create a premium experience.

Accessibility — We offer a generous free tier so everyone can benefit from better focus, with optional premium features for power users.
