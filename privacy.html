Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Introduction

Corelume Tech ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Focus Flow mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using Focus Flow, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally

Focus Flow stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Focus session history (duration, timestamps, tree species selected)
  • Forest data (planted trees, growth stages)
  • Habit tracking data (habits, completions, streaks)
  • App preferences (theme, language, sound settings, timer durations)
  • Notification settings

This data is stored using local databases and shared preferences on your device, and does not leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

Information Collected by Third Parties

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Firebase Analytics — Collects anonymous usage analytics such as session counts, screen views, and crash reports. This data is anonymized and used solely to improve the app experience. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Google AdMob — Displays ads within the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing — Processes in-app purchases and subscriptions. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google. We do not receive or store your payment details.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's core functionality
  • To improve user experience based on anonymous, aggregated analytics
  • To display advertisements to free-tier users
  • To process subscription purchases through Google Play
  • To detect, diagnose, and fix bugs and crashes

Data Storage & Security

Your personal productivity data (focus sessions, habits, forest) is stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to this data and cannot retrieve it.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the information processed by our third-party services. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Advertisements

Focus Flow displays advertisements through Google AdMob for free-tier users. You can remove all ads by subscribing to Focus Flow Pro. Ad personalization settings can be managed through your device's Google Account settings.

In-App Purchases

Focus Flow offers optional premium subscriptions (monthly and yearly plans). All payment processing is handled by Google Play Billing. We do not store, collect, or have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Focus Flow is not directed to children under 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Delete your data — Clear all local data by uninstalling the app or using the in-app data reset option
  • Opt out of analytics — Disable analytics collection through your device settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads — Adjust ad preferences in your Google Account settings or device privacy settings
  • Data portability — Your data is stored locally on your device and is under your control at all times
  • Contact us — Request information about data we process

International Users

Focus Flow is available globally. By using the App, you acknowledge that any information collected by third-party services may be processed in accordance with the laws of the jurisdictions where those services operate. We comply with applicable data protection regulations including the Information Technology Act, 2000 (India), GDPR (EU), and other applicable local privacy laws.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the App and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
