Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about playing free browser games at Corelume Tech.

Are all games really free?

Yes, every game on Corelume Tech is 100% free to play. No downloads, no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no pay-to-win mechanics. We sustain our platform through non-intrusive advertising.

Do I need to download anything?

No. All our games are HTML5 browser games that run directly in Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge. Just click "Play" and the game loads instantly. No executables, no plugins, no storage space used.

Can I play on my phone or tablet?

Yes! Our games are built with responsive design and work on any device with a modern browser — Windows, Mac, Linux, Chromebook, iPad, Android, and more.

How do I play with a friend?

War Tanks and Orb Clash support local 2-player multiplayer. Two players share the same device and keyboard. Player 1 uses one set of controls and Player 2 uses another. Just open the game and start playing together!

Are the games safe to play?

Absolutely. Browser games run in a sandboxed environment and cannot access your files or install anything on your computer. Our games don't require accounts and don't collect personal data.

How often do you release new games?

We focus on quality over quantity. We currently have four original games in our library, with new titles in active development. Follow our blog for announcements about upcoming releases and feature updates.
