Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Introduction

Corelume Tech ("we," "our," or "us") operates interactive web games (the "Games"), including War Tanks. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you play our HTML5 browser games.

By playing our Games, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Information Collected by Third Parties

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Analytics — Collects anonymous usage analytics such as player counts, session lengths, screen views, and crash reports. This data is anonymized and used solely to improve the gameplay experience.
  • Google AdSense — Displays ads within our web games. AdSense may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data (using cookies) to personalize advertisements as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the stability of our Games
  • To improve player experience based on anonymous, aggregated analytics
  • To display advertisements that keep our games free to play
  • To detect, diagnose, and fix bugs and crashes

Data Storage & Security

Our web games operate entirely in your browser and do not require you to create an account. No personal identifiable information (such as name, email, or address) is collected directly by Corelume Tech.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the information processed by our third-party services.

Advertisements

Our Games display advertisements through Google AdSense. Advertising allows us to keep our games 100% free. Ad personalization settings and cookies can be managed through your device's or browser's Google Account settings.

Children's Privacy

Our Games are designed to be family-friendly, but are not explicitly directed to children under 13 without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding the third-party platforms we use:

  • Manage Cookies — You can clear browser cookies or use incognito modes to reset ad identifiers
  • Opt out of personalized ads — Adjust ad preferences in your Google Account settings or browser privacy settings
  • Contact us — Request information about our privacy practices

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued playing constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us
