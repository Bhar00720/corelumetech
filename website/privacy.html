Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last updated: March 7, 2025

Introduction

Antigravity ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Focus Flow mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using Focus Flow, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally

Focus Flow stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Focus session history (duration, timestamps, tree species)
  • Forest data (planted trees, growth stages)
  • Habit tracking data (habits, completions, streaks)
  • App preferences (theme, language, sound settings, timer durations)
  • Notification settings

This data is stored using Hive database and SharedPreferences, and does not leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

Information Collected by Third Parties

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Firebase Analytics — Collects anonymous usage analytics such as session counts, screen views, and crash reports. This data is anonymized and used solely to improve the app experience.
  • Google AdMob — Displays ads within the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Google Play Billing — Processes in-app purchases and subscriptions. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google.
  • Firebase Remote Config — Fetches app configuration settings to customize the user experience. No personal data is transmitted.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To improve user experience based on anonymous analytics
  • To display relevant advertisements to free-tier users
  • To process subscription purchases
  • To send in-app notifications about features and updates
  • To detect and fix bugs and crashes

Data Storage & Security

Your personal productivity data (focus sessions, habits, forest) is stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to this data and cannot retrieve it.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the information processed by our third-party services. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

Advertisements

Focus Flow displays advertisements through Google AdMob for free-tier users. You may encounter:

  • Interstitial ads — Full-screen ads shown between screens
  • Rewarded ads — Optional ads you can watch for additional features

You can remove all ads by subscribing to Focus Flow Pro. Ad personalization settings can be managed through your device's Google Account settings.

In-App Purchases

Focus Flow offers optional premium subscriptions (monthly and yearly plans). All payment processing is handled by Google Play Billing. We do not store your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Focus Flow is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us to have it removed.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your data — Clear all local data by uninstalling the app or using the in-app data reset option
  • Opt out of analytics — Disable analytics through your device settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads — Adjust ad preferences in your Google Account settings
  • Contact us — Request information about data we process via our contact page

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the App and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
