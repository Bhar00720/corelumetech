Built by passionate gamers.

Hello! We are the Corelume Tech team. What started as a late-night weekend passion project to recreate the nostalgic feeling of early 2000s browser gaming has exploded into a full-scale web entertainment platform.

We grew tired of the modern mobile gaming ecosystem filled with intrusive ads, paywalls, and bloated 2GB downloads. We believe in the power of the open web. We write our games from scratch using precise mathematics, HTML5 Canvas, and pure JavaScript. Our mission is to keep gaming accessible, highly competitive, and absolutely frictionless.
