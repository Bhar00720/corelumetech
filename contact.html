How to Write a Great Bug Report

If you've encountered a bug or issue in one of our games, the more details you provide, the faster we can fix it. Here's a template you can use when emailing us:

Subject: Bug Report — [Game Name] Game: (e.g., War Tanks, Hex Blast, Sky Dodge, Orb Clash) Browser: (e.g., Chrome 120, Firefox 115, Safari 17) Device: (e.g., Windows 11 Desktop, iPad Air, Android Phone) What happened: Describe the issue clearly. What did you see? Steps to reproduce: 1. Open the game 2. Click on / do this... 3. The bug occurs when... Expected behavior: What should have happened instead? Screenshot (optional): Attach a screenshot if possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your games really free to play?

Yes, 100% free. All of our games — War Tanks, Hex Blast, Sky Dodge, and Orb Clash — are completely free to play in your browser. No downloads, no subscriptions, no hidden fees, and no pay-to-win mechanics. We sustain our platform through non-intrusive advertising.

Which browsers do your games support?

Our games work on any modern browser that supports HTML5 Canvas. This includes Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Apple Safari, and Microsoft Edge. We recommend using the latest version of your browser for the best performance. Both desktop and mobile browsers are fully supported.

How do I play games with a friend?

War Tanks and Orb Clash support local 2-player multiplayer. Two players share the same keyboard — Player 1 uses one set of controls and Player 2 uses another. Just open the game, and both players can start immediately. Online multiplayer is currently in development.

Can I request a new game or feature?

Absolutely! We love hearing game ideas and feature requests from our community. Send your suggestions to contact@corelumetech.in with the subject line "Game Suggestion" or "Feature Request." While we can't guarantee every idea will be implemented, we carefully consider all feedback.

Do you collect personal data?

No. Our games don't require accounts and don't collect personally identifiable information. Gameplay settings are stored locally in your browser. We use Google Analytics for anonymous usage statistics and Google AdSense for displaying advertisements. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

My game is lagging or running slowly. What can I do?

Try these steps: (1) Close other browser tabs to free up memory, (2) Make sure your browser is updated to the latest version, (3) Disable browser extensions that might interfere with Canvas rendering, (4) Try using Chrome for the best HTML5 Canvas performance. If issues persist, send us a bug report with your device and browser details.

Response Times

We aim to respond to all emails within 24-48 hours. Bug reports and player feedback are our highest priority. Partnership inquiries may take slightly longer as we evaluate each opportunity carefully.
